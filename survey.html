Are you appropriating culture?

Cultural appropriation is the "borrowing" of certain elements of a culture without getting consent from people of that culture. Cultural apropriation usually involves people of a more dominant racial and ethnic group exploting less privileged/oppressed cultures and people. This is a problem because the "borrowing" of cultures robs minorities of the credit they deserve. Take the quiz below to know what is not culturally acceptable for you to wear !
